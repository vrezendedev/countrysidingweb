Why is it "Development's"/"Developer's"?

Everything is about the development of the game but with different approaches. While being a "Development's" article, it'll show features or assets or technical or any kind of information about the game and its development - so it'll be, probably, way more straightforward. While being a "Developer's" article, it'll be a more personal approach about the game, for example: what I'm currently reading or studying about game design and how it impacts my decisions as the designer of this game... or, what I'm currently struggling at as the developer... or what I want to express in certain terms of something that is already added (or will be) in the game, like why Jasmine's (an NPC) personality is like that or why the player's character's harvesting animation is like that and what I, as an artist, want that to signify for the player.

Happy reading and enjoy the content :D
