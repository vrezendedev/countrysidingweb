F.A.Q

  • What do you pretend to achieve, for yourself, with Countrysiding?
    I want to prove myself that I can develop one of my dream games... and, of course, improve myself as a developer.
  • When will it be released?
    When it's done.
  • What language is Countrysiding coded in?
    C#.
  • What is your goal?
    To give remarkable experiences in people's lives as Stardew Valley, Minecraft, Dark Souls, Lord of the Rings Online, Skyrim and so many other titles gave me. And, of course, I want players to have fun.
  • What or Who inspires you?
    What: Gravity Falls, Stardew Valley, Lovecraftian myths, Minecraft, "countryside" culture...
    Who: Eric Barone, Ryan Dorin, Adam Younis...
  • Can I talk/post about your game on Social Media?
    Yes! Please! And I will always be grateful for that.
  • Will Countrysiding have controller support?
    Yes, I pretend to implement it.
  • What about translations?
    I can confirm, for now, English and Brazilian Portuguese.
  • What tools do you use?
    Unity, Aseprite, Visual Studio, and FL Studio.
  • Isn't it too early to create a website?
    Probably, but I truly believe that when we show our idea, talk about it, even name it... the chances of it becoming a real thing is way bigger.
